Self-diagnose yourself...

You may have experienced this situation often and require a doctor's assistance right away, however they are not available for a variety of reasons. The Disease Prediction system is a project for online user support and consulting. Here, It is a web application that would enable users to receive immediate advice on their illness from an intelligent system. Various data are provided into the programme, along with the diseases they are related with. Users of the programme can communicate difficulties relating to their diseases. After then, it examines user-specific information to look for various illnesses that could be connected to it. Here, we use data mining algorithms to identify the disease which is most likely to be connected to the patient's details.
